About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computational Mathematics at Michigan State University, specializing in predictive modeling for healthcare and high-dimensional simulations. My work involves advanced machine learning techniques applied to biomedical imaging and algorithm development.

With extensive experience in math education—from middle school to collegiate levels—I develop innovative, project-based curricula that integrate data science and computational methods. Additionally, I am an avid strength athlete, competing in amateur strongman competitions.
