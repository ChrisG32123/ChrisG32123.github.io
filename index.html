About Me

I am a PhD candidate in data science with a strong focus on developing algorithms for longitudinal biomedical data. My work includes predicting brain cancer recurrence using multimodal MRI data, optimizing mechanical ventilation weaning for NICU patients, and mentoring undergraduate researchers. I am passionate about applying advanced machine learning techniques to solve real-world biomedical problems.

Projects

  • Mechanical Ventilation Optimization: Developing a model to optimize the weaning process for neonatal ICU patients.
  • MRI Brain Cancer Recurrence Prediction: Using multimodal MRI data to predict brain cancer recurrence in collaboration with Michigan State University.
  • Plasma Simulations: Conducted advanced plasma simulations using FFT and correlation models in hydrodynamics.
  • Powerlifting Programming: Created custom powerlifting programs for fitness and strength progression, with a focus on rowing strength and work capacity.

Research

My research focuses on applying machine learning models to biomedical data, especially in longitudinal settings. Current research includes:

  • Predictive Models for Brain Cancer Recurrence: Developing advanced machine learning models to predict recurrence based on MRI data.
  • NICU Mechanical Ventilation Optimization: Improving the mechanical ventilation process for NICU patients using time-series analysis and synthetic data generation.

Teaching

I have extensive experience teaching math at the K-8 level, including advanced math courses using Houghton Mifflin's Math in Focus curriculum. I mentor students in various levels of math and SAT preparation, and I am passionate about fostering a love for learning in my students.

Powerlifting

In addition to my academic and research work, I am a dedicated powerlifter, focusing on strength, conditioning, and work capacity. I have developed custom programs to increase rowing strength and enhance overall fitness. My training philosophy is based on balancing heavy lifts with unilateral work, conditioning, and explosive movements.
