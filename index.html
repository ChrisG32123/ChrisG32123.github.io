About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computational Mathematics at Michigan State University, specializing in predictive modeling for healthcare and high-dimensional simulations. My work involves machine learning techniques applied to longitudinal biomedical imaging, plasma simulations, and data-driven algorithm development.

Projects

  • Brain Cancer Progression Modeling: Used LSTM-based models on MRI data, achieving state-of-the-art performance.
  • NICU Ventilator Decision Support: Developed machine learning tools to assist clinicians in optimizing patient care.
  • Plasma Simulations: Advanced modeling and forecasting techniques for high-dimensional plasma systems.
